HOW WAS THE APP CREATED AND HOW DO YOU USE IT?

This app was created using stackblitz.com with the use of the Ionic Typescript. The app was created in order to provide users with a simple and quick method in calculating the Total Interest Payable, Monthly Amount, and Total Amount due in a Credit Card Balance Transfer. In order to use the app users must simply input their Balance Transfer Amount and Payment Terms.
